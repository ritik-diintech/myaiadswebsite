# myAiAds App Overview

## 📱 About the App
**myAiAds** is a cutting-edge mobile application designed to revolutionize the way businesses create advertisements. Built with **Flutter**, it leverages artificial intelligence to help users generate professional-grade video ads with minimal effort. The app specifically targets high-impact industries like Real Estate and Consumer Goods, providing them with sophisticated tools to showcase their products and services.

---

## 🚀 Key Features

### 1. AI-Powered Video Generation
The core feature of the app is its ability to use AI to craft compelling video advertisements. It simplifies the production process, making high-end marketing accessible to everyone.

### 2. Category-Specific Templates
The app offers specialized templates and assets for:
*   **Real Estate:** Professional cinematic tours and property highlight reels.
*   **Consumer Goods:** Dynamic product showcases and e-commerce ads.
*   **Custom Categories:** Flexible options for various business needs.

### 3. High-Quality Media Assets
The app comes pre-loaded with high-resolution video templates (`real_state`, `consumer`, etc.) and optimized images to ensure every ad looks premium.

### 4. Seamless User Experience
*   **Google One-Tap Login:** Easy and secure authentication using Google accounts.
*   **Interactive Splash:** A smooth, video-based splash screen (`splash_video.mp4`) that sets a modern tone from the start.
*   **Firebase Integration:** Real-time data syncing and secure cloud storage.

Create video ads and reels using AI tools for business content.

My AI Ads is an AI-powered tool designed to help users create marketing videos using virtual avatars.

The app allows users to convert text or scripts into short video ads suitable for social media platforms. It provides templates, avatar-based presentation, and automated video generation to simplify content creation.

Key Features:

AI Video Generation
Create marketing videos from text input without requiring video editing skills or recording equipment.

Template Library
Choose from a variety of pre-designed templates suitable for platforms such as Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts, and TikTok.

AI Avatars
Generate videos with virtual avatars that present your content with synchronized voice and animation.

Content Use Cases:

* E-commerce: Present product highlights in video format
* Services: Explain offerings using avatar-based videos
* Real Estate: Create property-related promotional videos
* Education: Share informational or learning content

Additional Features:

* Dashboard for managing generated videos
* Automatic formatting for vertical video platforms
* Fast video processing
* Secure handling of user data and generated content

How It Works:

1. Select a template
2. Add your script or text
3. Generate the video
4. Download and share on your preferred platform

My AI Ads is designed to assist individuals and businesses in creating video-based marketing content more efficiently.
